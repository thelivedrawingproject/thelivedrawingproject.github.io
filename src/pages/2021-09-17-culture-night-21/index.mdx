#### Description

We are coming back a second year for Culture Night! We will project on the National Gallery of Dublin and at the Irish Cultural Center in Paris simultaneously. 
Participants are able to see their drawings on both sides, creating exchanges between France and Ireland. The drawings origin countries will be displayed on the projection.

During the event, 8 illustrators will perform to draw on the walls, on site or from their home.
